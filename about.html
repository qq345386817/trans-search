Product overview

A small tool for broader search.

Translate Before Search is built for moments when the best answer may exist in another language, but switching your query manually every time is too much friction.

What it does

On supported search pages, the extension can auto-translate your query into a target language before opening results. You can also switch to manual-only control when you want more control.

What it is for

It helps with multilingual research, browsing, learning, and everyday searching when relevant information may be written in a different language.

What it is not

It is not trying to replace your search engine or force automation everywhere. The goal is to reduce friction while letting you decide when translation should step in.

Core controls

  • Set a target language for translated searches.
  • Switch quickly between recent target languages.
  • Open translated results in the current tab or a new tab.
  • Skip searches that already appear to be in the target language.
  • Keep selected sites in manual-only mode.
  • Exclude specific query languages from auto-translation.

For setup steps and troubleshooting, go to Help.
