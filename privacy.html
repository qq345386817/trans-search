Privacy

Privacy policy

Translate Before Search is designed to do its job with a minimal, practical approach to data use.

What the extension uses

The extension reads the current supported search page URL and query so it can determine whether translation should run and build the translated search request.

Your settings, such as target language and site preferences, are stored locally in the browser extension storage.

What is not collected for its own sake

Translate Before Search does not treat your searches as a user profile, does not provide a social feed, and is not designed around selling personal data.

Third-party services

Search results are opened on third-party search engines, and translation may rely on browser or platform capabilities. Those services may process requests according to their own policies.
